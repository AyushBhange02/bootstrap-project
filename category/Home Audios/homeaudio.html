The best Speakers for the ultimate home entertainment

A good set of Speakers can help you turn mundane movie nights into something cinematic. Whether you're looking for something portable, robust or heavy-duty, Speakers today cater to every need and are available in a variety of forms. If choosing one seems overwhelming, it is best to begin with the question most asked -- wired or wireless? 


Should you opt for a wired Speaker or a Bluetooth Speaker?

Both Wired and Wireless Speakers are an excellent investment if you prefer unparalleled sound quality. Wired Speakers typically offer superior sound quality, as it is easier to transmit a large amount of electrical data through a wire as compared to Bluetooth connectivity. However, if you're the kind that prefers moving about and likes to carry their party with them, a Portable Bluetooth Speaker is your best bet. 


What are some features of a Speaker?

Speakers today are built to withstand extremes and play for long hours. With seamless Bluetooth and Wi-Fi connectivity, you can choose to integrate your Speaker with your Television or simply use it with a Smartphone to amplify its sound. You can also invest in a Smart Speaker that can act as a hub and run-on voice commands to perform day-to-day tasks.


You can also invest in a Party Speaker that comes fired up with exceptional acoustics and powerful bass. Despite their heavy-duty build, they are portable and make excellent travel companions on longer journeys. If your primary use involves catching up on your favourite show, opting for a Soundbar or Tower Speakers can help you enjoy a 360-degree experience within the four walls of your living room.
